Business intelligence

Business Intelligence (BI) is a data management discipline that aims to extract, analyze and present useful business information for decision-making. It involves the use of software and technology to collect, integrate and analyze data from different sources, to provide valuable information for planning, budgeting, forecasting and performance monitoring purposes. BI enables businesses to make informed decisions in real time by identifying trends, patterns and relationships hidden in data. Companies can use BI to improve operational efficiency, optimize business processes, increase profitability, and gain competitive advantage.

Data Warehouse

The Data Warehouse is a database optimized for analytical queries and report generation that stores and analyzes large amounts of data from different sources. Data is collected from sources such as transactional databases, flat files, and line-of-business applications, then transformed and loaded into the data warehouse in a consistent format to enable in-depth analysis and data-driven decision making. precise data. Businesses use data warehouses to store and analyze historical data, as well as to create dashboards, reports, and analytics to help with strategic decision making. The data warehouse is a key part of the Business Intelligence (BI) infrastructure and can help companies better understand their performance and identify opportunities for improvement.

ETL

Le processus ETL (Extraction, Transformation, Chargement) est utilisé dans les systèmes de Business Intelligence (BI) pour collecter des données à partir de différentes sources externes, nettoyer et préparer ces données pour l'analyse, puis les stocker dans une base de données ou un entrepôt de données pour une utilisation ultérieure. Les outils ETL facilitent ce processus et permettent aux entreprises d'accéder à des informations précieuses pour prendre des décisions plus éclairées.
